# WEBSITE REQUIREMENTS

## [REQ-SITECOPY] Website Copy Conventions

- The website MUST use first-person plural language even when the company is currently operated by one person.
- The website MUST explain somewhere in its content that this plural language is intentional and reflects optimistic future-proofing and AI-agent inclusiveness rather than an attempt to misrepresent company size.
- The homepage hero line MUST be exactly: `Software should foster the user's agency, autonomy, and understanding.`
- The homepage MAY include forward-looking vision and future-oriented direction.
- The About page MUST stay grounded in the past and present.
- The About page MAY describe current work, current interests, and current beliefs, but it MUST not rely on speculative future projections as its main substance.

## [REQ-SITESTRUCTURE] Website Structure

- The public company site MUST publish the homepage at `/`.
- It MUST publish an About page at `/about/`.
- It MUST publish a Contact page at `/contact/`.
- The homepage at `/` MUST carry the site's primary company messaging.
- Beyond `/about/` and `/contact/`, additional company-level standalone pages MUST NOT be published unless another requirement explicitly requires them.
- App-specific pages under their own section paths are allowed when required elsewhere in the contract set.

## [REQ-DRIPWORDSLEGAL] Dripwords Legal Page

- The site MUST include the file `content/dripwords/privacy.md`.
