## Privacy

Dripwords does not require an account. We do not intentionally collect, store, or sell your personal data on our own servers.

The app is designed so that your data stays on your device.

## Local data

Any reading progress, preferences, or other app data created while using Dripwords is stored locally on your device.

We do not operate a backend service for Dripwords user data.

## Syncing across devices

If you use Dripwords features that sync data across devices, the app may use Google's Wearable DataLayer. In that case, some app data needed for syncing may pass through Google's infrastructure.

We do not control how Google processes data within those services. If you use those features, you should also review Google's own privacy terms and policies.

## Purchases and refunds

If you buy Dripwords through Google Play, the purchase is processed by Google Play.

We do not issue direct refunds for Google Play purchases. If you want a refund, you must request it through Google Play and it will be handled according to Google's refund rules, deadlines, and decisions.
