## Last updated

March 1, 2025

## 1. Information Collection

Dripwords does not collect any personal data or user information. We do not require account registration, nor do we track, store, or share any information about you. All content provided by the app consists solely of bundled public domain literary texts.

## 2. Data Storage and Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no user information stored on our servers. Any data generated during your use of the app remains solely on your device.

## 3. Third-Party Services

Dripwords does not integrate with any third-party services that collect or share your personal data. However, please note that Dripwords uses Google's Wearable DataLayer for syncing data across devices. This syncing mechanism may transmit some data through Google's servers. We recommend reviewing Google's privacy policy for more details on how your data is handled.

## 4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.

## 5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: [contact@kymasoft.com](mailto:contact@kymasoft.com)
