KYMASOFT is currently operated by one person, but we use first-person plural language throughout this site on purpose. It reflects optimistic future-proofing and AI-agent inclusiveness, not an attempt to misrepresent company size.

We are currently experimenting with the web because of its openness and with mobile because of how widely it reaches people. We are also measuring the current limits of agentic AI tools in practical software work.

Ultimately, what we are exploring is how, and to what extent, those ideas can be turned into reality through actual software engineering work.

From a software engineering perspective, the values behind that work are human- and user-aligned augmentation, educational value, decentralized and sovereignty-friendly architectures, adaptability to changing realities, and awareness of the broader impact that software has on people and society.
